about me.

hey there! I’m Anna - I’m a senior studying Computer Science 💻 (major) and Art 🎨 (minor) at Northwestern University (📍Evanston, IL). I've interned (and freelanced) for Albert.io, and I'm looking forward to joining the Disney Streaming Services team after I graduate.

Alongside my interest for technology, I’ve always had a passion for education, art, and for making positive social impact 💛.

Through the past decade 🕙, I’ve worked as an educator through various activites: tutoring, TA-ing Northwestern CS courses, and teaching my own Web Design enrichment courses to name a few 👩🏼‍🏫.

To quench my need to "do good", I co-founded Northwestern’s Develop + Innovate for Social Change, now working as the Tech Studio Lead 🙇🏼‍♀️. I am also NU’s Women in Computing Internal President, one of the most fulfilling experiences I’ve had at my time in college 👁❤️👩🏼‍💻.
